About Muzinda Muzinda is a technological learning and Innovation Hub What Do We Want To Achieve? To develop a platform that will empower young Zimbabweans with relevant, 21st century digital skills & entrepreneurial opportunities How Will We Get There? Training and equipping young people with world class business and digital skills Providing a core working place for community engagement & Colloboration Creating a platform that connects young people to economic opportunities within the emerging digital and knowledge economy. What We Do? Muzinda Hub is much more than just a skills development centre of excellence; it is an establishment that aims to match the youths burgeoning tech skills with the requirements of the digital economy. Muzinda will provide quality online/offline training that teaches high-demand digital such as Social Media, iOS, Android, HTML, CSS, WordPress, PHP, Python, Java- Script, Ruby, and more. Top candidates will be identified and given career services, mentoring, and additional training to be vetted as job-ready.
